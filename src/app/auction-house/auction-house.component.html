What is the auction house page?

At its core, the auction house page is a tool to search the WoW auction house online. You can also compare prices of different realms by navigating to an item and switching the realm. We will update the prices for you.

But we are just at the beginning. We will be providing you with a lot more features in the future. So be sure to check back regulary!

Do you support all realms?

We support all realms on the eu and us regions. You can pick your realm with the button under the search bar. Be sure to pick your region before picking your realm!

How often do you update your data?

We usually update all auction information every hour. We get this information directly from Blizzard through their API. Still, we can unfortunately not 100% guarantee the correctness of the information.
