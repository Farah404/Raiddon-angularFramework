Inscription

Inscription is a profession which was introduced in World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King Patch 3.0.2. Inscription allows players to create glyphs, scrolls, Darkmoon cards, vellum and various off-hand tomes. Inscription includes a "gathering" skill: milling. This skill creates base materials from herbs, and is similar to disenchanting and prospecting.

Inscription is taught by various inscription trainers located throughout the world. Prospective scribes must be level 5. Initial training costs 10 copper, granting the Apprentice level with a potential skill of 75. Inscription Trainers are available in every racial capital, allowing players to train in all proficiencies. Trainers are also available in World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade Hellfire Peninsula and World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King Northrend.
